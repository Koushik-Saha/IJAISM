# Data Retention Policy

## 1. Overview
This policy outlines how long IJAISM retains user data and the procedures for data deletion.

## 2. Retention Periods

### 2.1 User Accounts
- **Active Accounts**: Retained indefinitely while active.
- **Inactive Accounts**: Retained for 5 years after last login, then notified/archived.
- **Deleted Accounts**: Personal identifiers scrubbed immediately. Transactional records (Audit logs) retained for 7 years for legal compliance.

### 2.2 Published Articles
- Published articles are part of the scientific record and are retained **permanently**.
- Authorship metadata associated with published works cannot be erased but can be corrected.

### 2.3 Review Data
- Review comments and decision letters are retained for **10 years** to support editorial integrity.

### 2.4 Payment Records
- Retained for **7 years** as required by tax and accounting laws.

## 3. Data Deletion
Users may request deletion via the "Delete Account" option in their dashboard or by emailing support.
- **Hard Deletion**: Removes all records (except published articles/payments).
- **Soft Deletion**: Anonymizes personal data but keeps statistical records.
